Dr. Kishore Lal

Director & Chief Mentor (Visionary Leader Guiding ASRO’s Journey)

Dr. Kishore Lal, recognized as the Grand Master of Intuition and a world-record holder, leads ASRO’s multidisciplinary vision where science, innovation, and human potential meet. He has guided students to achieve 53 World Records through pioneering training methods in meditation, memory, and intuitive learning — including extraordinary blindfolded performances.

As a mentor and space education leader, he has also shaped ASRO’s Space Wing, giving students unique exposure to Space Science, Technology, and Artificial Intelligence. His mission is to inspire young minds, blending advanced learning with exploration, and empowering the next generation to carry India’s space ambitions to new frontiers.

PRADEEPKUMAR P

DEPUTY HEAD, ASCD, AVN, VSSC (Retd.). DIGITAL SYSTEMS GROUP, VIKRAM SARABHAI SPACE CENTRE ISRO, DEPT.OF SPACE, GOVT OF INDIA, TRIVANDRUM.

PRADEEPKUMAR P has significantly contributed to multiple planetary mission payloads and scientific instruments for ISRO, including:
(Chandrayaan-I) – First direct detection of water in lunar ambience with mass spectrometer coverage for altitude & latitude.
MENCA payload (Mars Orbiter Mission) – Designed and developed the onboard system for operation and telecommand.
CHACE-2 payload (Chandrayaan-2) – Onboard system design for telecommand-based operation from ground.
Payload (Chandrayaan-2 & 3 Lander) – Design and operation control from ground.
Design of onboard systems for scientific payloads of upcoming Venus & MOM-2 missions.

    Manager, MENCA Payload onboard Mars Orbiter Mission
  • Experience:37+ years in Data Acquisition & Control Systems for Planetary Missions
  • Education:M Tech in Applied Electronics and Instrumentation, B Tech in Electronics and Communication Engineering and Diploma in Electronics Engineering.
